Activities

Bruin Supermileage EV

As a member of the Electronics Subteam, I worked on designing and building a high-efficiency electric vehicle. My responsibilities included developing the PCB to encode steering wheel switch behavior, integrating the Controller Area Network (CAN) Bus, and generating fabrication files. This hands-on experience allowed me to apply my engineering skills in a real-world project and collaborate with a diverse team of fellow students.

IEEE at UCLA

As an engaged member of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) at UCLA, I actively participated in technical workshops, hackathons, and networking events. I also served as a member of the organizing committee for IEEE-sponsored events, gaining valuable experience in event planning, team coordination, technical presentations, and fostering a collaborative learning environment.

Rocket project at UCLA

As a member of the Rocket Project, I contributed to the design and implementation of electrical systems, including avionics and telemetry, for high-powered rocket launches. Through this experience, I gained valuable insights into aerospace engineering and developed skills in problem-solving and teamwork in a challenging and dynamic project environment.

Sigma Phi Epsilon Learning Community

As a member of the Sigma Phi Epsilon Learning Community, I participated in a specialized program focused on academic excellence, leadership development, and community engagement. I engaged in various activities such as workshops, community service, and leadership training, which helped me develop valuable skills in time management, communication, and teamwork.
